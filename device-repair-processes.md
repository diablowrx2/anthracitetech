## Universal Repair Workflow

Every repair — regardless of device type — follows this core 4-step process:

### Step 1: Intake & Free Diagnostic
- Customer books via the website form, phone, or walk-in.
- A technician performs a **free diagnostic** (no obligation, no hidden fees).
- The device is inspected, the issue is identified, and a firm quote is provided **before any work begins**.
- Customer approves or declines the repair. No pressure.

### Step 2: Repair Authorization & Scheduling
- If approved, the repair is queued based on urgency level and service type.
- For in-store: the device is logged into the system with a ticket ID (e.g., `EZT-XXXXXX`).
- For house calls: a technician is dispatched within the quoted window.
- For mail-in: shipping instructions are sent; device is received and logged.
- For remote: a secure screen-share link is emailed to the customer.

### Step 3: Repair Execution
- The technician performs the authorized repair using OEM or high-quality replacement parts.
- Customer can watch (in-store), observe via screen-share (remote), or receive progress updates (mail-in/house call).
- Post-repair testing is performed to verify functionality.

### Step 4: Completion, Warranty & Follow-Up
- Device is returned to the customer (or session ends for remote).
- Customer receives a repair summary and warranty documentation.
- **Warranty**: 30 days (Essential Fix), 90 days (Complete Care), or as specified.
- Post-service follow-up email may be sent to confirm satisfaction.

## Remote-Only Services

Some issues do not require physical access and can be resolved entirely over a secure remote connection.

### Capabilities
- Virus & malware removal
- PC tune-up & speed optimization
- Network & WiFi troubleshooting
- Software installation & configuration
- Cloud & backup setup
- Password resets & account recovery
- Printer & peripheral setup

### Step-by-Step Process

1. **Book Your Session**
   - Customer fills out the remote booking form, selects timezone, and picks a time slot.
   - Same-day appointments available for urgent issues.

2. **Connect Securely**
   - Customer receives a secure screen-share link via email or text.
   - No permanent software download required — a small temporary helper app may run during the session and auto-removes on disconnect.
   - Connection uses TLS 1.3 end-to-end encryption.
   - Customer must explicitly approve the connection and can disconnect instantly at any time.

3. **Diagnose & Fix**
   - Technician sees the customer's screen, identifies the issue, and repairs it in real time.
   - Customer watches every action and can chat or ask questions throughout.
   - For issues requiring hands-on repair (physical damage, hardware replacement), technician provides a quote for in-store, house call, or mail-in service.

4. **Session Complete**
   - Technician disconnects; nothing remains on the customer's computer.
   - Customer receives a summary of fixes applied and prevention tips.
