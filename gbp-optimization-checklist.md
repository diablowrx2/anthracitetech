## 5. Q&A Management

### Proactive Q&A Setup
Pre-populate common questions before customers ask them. Ask and answer these from the owner account:

- [ ] **Q:** Do you offer same-day repairs?  
  **A:** Yes! Most screen replacements, battery swaps, and diagnostics are completed within 1–2 hours. Walk-ins are welcome during business hours (Mon–Fri 9–7, Sat 10–5).

- [ ] **Q:** Do you repair game consoles like PlayStation and Xbox?  
  **A:** Absolutely. We handle HDMI port repairs, disc drive issues, overheating fixes, and internal cleaning for PS4, PS5, Xbox One, and Xbox Series X/S. Typical turnaround is same-day to 24 hours.

- [ ] **Q:** Is there a diagnostic fee?  
  **A:** No — diagnostics are completely free. We'll inspect your device, explain the issue, and give you a firm quote before any work begins. No pressure, no hidden fees.

- [ ] **Q:** Do you make house calls?  
  **A:** Yes! We offer mobile repair service in Harrisburg, Camp Hill, Mechanicsburg, and Midtown. There is a $50 house call fee in addition to the repair cost. Perfect for busy professionals or desktop PC issues.

- [ ] **Q:** What types of phones do you repair?  
  **A:** iPhone, Samsung Galaxy, Google Pixel, and most Android devices. Common repairs include screen replacement, battery replacement, charging port repair, and water damage assessment.

- [ ] **Q:** Do you offer any warranty?  
  **A:** Yes — all repairs come with a 90-day warranty on parts and labor. If the same issue reoccurs, we'll make it right at no extra charge.

- [ ] **Q:** Can you recover data from a dead laptop?  
  **A:** In many cases, yes. We specialize in data recovery from failed hard drives, SSDs, and unresponsive laptops. Bring it in for a free diagnostic and we'll assess recoverability.

### Ongoing Q&A Monitoring
- [ ] Check GBP Q&A section **daily** for new questions
- [ ] Respond to all new questions within **4 hours** during business hours
- [ ] Upvote helpful answers (both your own and positive customer answers)
- [ ] Flag and report inaccurate answers from the public
- [ ] Update answers if policies, hours, or services change

### Q&A Response Guidelines
- [ ] Always include a soft CTA ("Stop by," "Give us a call," "Book online")
- [ ] Mention location naturally ("at our Harrisburg shop")
- [ ] Keep answers concise but complete (2–4 sentences)
- [ ] Use keywords naturally ("computer repair," "iPhone screen replacement")


### Review Request SMS Template

```
Hi [Customer Name]! This is Anthracite Tech Co.. Thanks for letting us fix your [Device]. If you're happy with the service, would you mind leaving us a quick Google review? It really helps us out: [DIRECT_REVIEW_LINK] 🙏
```

### Review Request Best Practices
- [ ] Send request within 24–48 hours of completed repair (while experience is fresh)
- [ ] Use the direct review link — never ask customers to "search for us on Google"
- [ ] Keep SMS under 160 characters when possible to avoid split messages
- [ ] Include device/service reference for personalization
- [ ] Follow up once if no response after 7 days; stop after second request
- [ ] Offer a small incentive only if compliant with platform guidelines (e.g., "Leave a review and get 10% off your next repair" — verify against Google's policies)

---

### Response Time Targets
- [ ] Respond to **all reviews** within 24 hours
